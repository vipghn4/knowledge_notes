# Writing
**Examiner expectations**.
* *Band 6*. Use a mix of simple and complex sentences
* *Band 7*. Use a variety of complex structures

**Steps to more impressive writing**.
1. Master compound and complex sentences
2. Learn to use infinitives (to V) and gerunds (V-ing)
3. Add color to your writing with advanced verbs
    * Use irregular verbs
    * Use phrasal verbs
4. Change the focus (emphasis) with the passive voice
    * *Active voice*. Lisa made a phone call (pay attention to subject)
    * *Passive voice*. A phone call was made by Lisa (pay attention to object)

## Grammar
**Complex sentences**. Two or more simple sentences put together to make the essay more coherent and cohesive

>**NOTE**. Complex sentences do not mean complicated, long, or impressive

* *Examples*.
    * One of the most common environmental issues is global warming, which causes many serious environmental problems
    * There are tough challanges with this issue and its effects have very serious consequences
* *When to use compelx sentences*. 
    * *Simple sentences*. When we are making main points, normally at the beginning of a paragraph
    * *Complex sentences*. When we are expanding on the main points
* *Making complex sentences*. Use various grammatical structures allowing us to do that
    * *Idea*. Have an independent clause and a dependent clause
